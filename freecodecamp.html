Rev.Engineer Shem M.K Wanjila

A Father to many, a family man Teacher, and a Mentor.

  • 1949 -Born in Bunuku village, Malava County, Kakamega District.
  • 1964 -Joined Sigalaga Technical
  • 1966 -left Sigalagala Technical
  • June 1975 -Started Working at Telkom kenya
  • 1976 -Graduated from Technical University of Kenya
  • March 2004 -Retired from Telkom kenya
  • 2011- Joined Railway Training Institute as a lecturer
  • 2012- Joined PIEA School of Petroleum Studies as a lecturer
  • 2015- ordained as a Revrend pentecoastal Assemblies of kenya (PAG) K
